How It Works

Four Steps to Mastery

From raw attempt to exam-ready — here's the PRAJNA flow.

1

Attempt Exam

Take chapter or full-length tests on the PW platform — individual or centre-administered.

2

AI Analysis

The SLM processes your responses, time-per-question, accuracy patterns and peer data.

3

PRAJNA Score

You receive a 0–100 multi-dimensional score mapped to one of five performance tiers.

Smart Study Plan
